overview

PeaJust Illustration is all about the redemption of peace and justice in our societies. The community nowadays, does not seem to appreciate simple things, and tend to look for grandiose events. As the hope and future of our mother Earth, we shall unite and work as one to develop our surroundings.

objective

PeaJust Illustration aims to provide additional knowledge about our current dilemmas. Hundreds of people are piling up, asking and confused as to where their much needed justice are being given to. As young scholars, we aspire to promote equality and comfort amongst everyone in need.
